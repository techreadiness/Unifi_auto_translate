# Launching on Unifi Apps

## Unifi Apps Overview

### What is Unifi Apps?

Unifi Apps enables projects to access over 200M+ LINE Messenger users, allowing them to seamlessly experience the full Web3 journey—from wallet creation to using decentralized applications, earning stablecoin rewards through missions, trading tokens and NFTs, and discovering and sharing apps with friends—all within the LINE Messenger and web environment.

We collaborate with projects that leverage our infrastructure to acquire users on LINE, capture user attention at scale, and become an integral part of users’ everyday digital experiences. Through Unifi Apps, partners can tap into a mass user base powered by our stablecoin-based reward infrastructure, driving sustainable growth in close collaboration with the LINE and Kaia ecosystems.

### Who can Apply?

Unifi Apps is designed for projects that aim to create meaningful value within the LINE and Kaia ecosystems. Applicants are expected to meet the following criteria:

* Projects with clear plans to distribute user rewards
* A strong and sustainable monetization model
* Commitment to building a Kaia-native application
* Well-defined KPIs and long-term growth objectives within the ecosystem
* Demonstrated uniqueness and innovation compared to existing apps
* Ability to execute effective local and/or global marketing initiatives

### Unifi Apps Benefits

We offer marketing and ecosystem support to help partners reach users across LINE and the broader Web3 landscape. Support includes:

* Social media exposure and curated marketing campaigns
* Dedicated technical support for onboarding and integration
* Access to gas fee delegation programs
* Tokenomics and ecosystem guidance in collaboration with the Kaia Foundation

Through Unifi Apps, partners gain not only distribution at scale, but also the tools and support needed to build, grow, and succeed within one of Asia’s largest messaging-based ecosystems.
